INDONESIA

Although it is the 4th most populated country in the world, and a major tourist destination, Indonesia comes in at number 1. Located between Australia and Southeast Asia, Inodnesia is a large group of islands.


Made up of over 13,000 islands, Indonesia is the world's largest archipelago. Indonesia, also has more volcanoes than any other country in the world, 80 of them are active.


Komodo dragons, a species of lizard, are native to Indonesia and are found nowhere else naturally in the world.

Indonesia is one of the most biodiverse countries in the world. Over 10% of all flowering plants, birds, mammals, and reptile species are native to this country.
