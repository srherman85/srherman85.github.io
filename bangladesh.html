BANGLADESH

Bangladesh is the most densely populated country in the world, with a land area greater than 500 sq mi.


With a large population and small land area, there are around 3,000 citizens of Bangladesh for every square mile.

Bangladesh is located at the intersection of three large rivers: Ganges, Brahmaputra, and Meghna.


Bangladesh is home to the largest mangrove forest in the world: The Sundarbans Reserve Forest.
