CANADA

It is surprising that Canada is on this list, because it is the second largest country in the world, behind Russia.


Canada has more lakes than any other country in the world. Despite being smaller than Russia, there are almost four times as many lakes in Canada. Nine percent of Canada's area is made of freshwater lakes.

Mt. Logan is the tallest mountain in Canada and is located near the Alaska border.


The largest land border is the Canadian-American border, stretching from Washington to Maine.
