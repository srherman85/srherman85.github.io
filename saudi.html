SAUDI ARABIA

Saudi Arabia will start the construction of the Mukaab in its capital city's center. This skyscraper will be a cube over 1300 ft on all sides. Construction is planned to begin in 2024.


Saudi Arabia is home to the largest oil reserves, making it an economic global power.


It is also the largest producer of oil, which makes sense because the largest oil reserves are found there.

The Empty Quarter, pictured above, is the largest continuous sand desert in the world.


Mecca and Medina, two important cities in Islam, are located in Saudi Arabia.
